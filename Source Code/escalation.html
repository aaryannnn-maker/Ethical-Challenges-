Escalation of Conflicts

The Challenge

AI systems can react at superhuman speeds to perceived threats, increasing the risk of rapid, uncontrollable escalation during cyber conflicts. A small incident could snowball into full-scale warfare.

Solutions

  • Introduce mandatory "human pause" protocols before major cyber actions.
  • Develop AI de-escalation strategies focused on peaceful conflict resolution.
  • Negotiate international treaties on cyber conflict management involving AI.
