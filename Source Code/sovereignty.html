Violation of Sovereignty

The Challenge

AI-driven cyberattacks can cross borders invisibly, breaching national sovereignty without traditional warfare. This challenges the foundations of international law and national self-determination.

Solutions

  • Establish international norms prohibiting unauthorized AI cyber intrusions.
  • Enhance diplomatic cooperation to hold violators accountable under global law.
  • Develop defensive AI to protect national cyber borders effectively and ethically.
