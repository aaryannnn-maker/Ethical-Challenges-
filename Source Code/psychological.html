Psychological Warfare and Disinformation

The Challenge

AI-powered disinformation campaigns can manipulate public opinion, destabilize societies, and undermine democratic institutions. In cyber warfare, psychological attacks become strategic weapons without physical violence.

Solutions

  • Create AI systems capable of detecting and neutralizing fake content automatically.
  • Educate the public about recognizing and resisting disinformation tactics.
  • Implement strong penalties for state-sponsored disinformation campaigns.
