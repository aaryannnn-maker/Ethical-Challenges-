Ethical Challenges in AI-based Cyber Warfare

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is changing the face of cyber warfare by increasing the speed, stealth, and sophistication of attacks. However, this evolution comes with a host of ethical concerns. How can nations and technologists ensure that AI is used responsibly? Key issues like loss of human control, bias in decision-making, and threats to international stability demand urgent attention. Navigating these challenges is critical to ensuring that technology strengthens, rather than undermines, global security.
