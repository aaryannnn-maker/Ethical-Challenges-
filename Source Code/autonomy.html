Lack of Human Control (Autonomy)

The Challenge

Autonomous AI systems in cyber warfare can make critical decisions — including launching attacks — without human oversight. This loss of human control risks unintended escalations, civilian harm, and accountability gaps.

Solutions

  • Implement strict human-in-the-loop (HITL) requirements for any decision involving use of force.
  • Mandate international legal frameworks that ensure human accountability for AI actions.
  • Develop ethical guidelines emphasizing human judgment in all military AI operations.
