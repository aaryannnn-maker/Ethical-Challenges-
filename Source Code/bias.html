Bias and Discrimination

The Challenge

AI systems trained on biased data can make discriminatory decisions, disproportionately affecting vulnerable populations during cyber conflicts. This erodes trust and violates human rights.

Solutions

  • Enforce diverse, bias-free datasets during AI training.
  • Implement independent bias auditing for all AI systems used in military applications.
  • Promote ethical AI development focused on fairness, inclusivity, and equal protection.
