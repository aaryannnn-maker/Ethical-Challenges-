Distinction Between Civilians and Combatants

The Challenge

AI systems often struggle to differentiate between military and civilian targets, especially in cyberspace where infrastructure overlaps. Misidentification can lead to devastating consequences for innocent populations.

Solutions

  • Develop more advanced identification algorithms with rigorous accuracy standards.
  • Adopt policies requiring exhaustive verification before any offensive cyber operation.
  • Promote adherence to International Humanitarian Law (IHL) standards for AI use in war.
