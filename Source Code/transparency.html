Lack of Transparency (Black Box Problem)

The Challenge

Many AI systems operate as "black boxes," meaning their decision-making processes are opaque even to their creators. In cyber warfare, this lack of transparency hinders accountability, trust, and legal compliance.

Solutions

  • Mandate explainability in AI systems used for defense and cyber operations.
  • Invest in research for transparent and interpretable machine learning models.
  • Create auditing mechanisms to regularly evaluate AI systems for fairness and compliance.
